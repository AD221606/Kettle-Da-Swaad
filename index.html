Boiled Eggs

Ingredients required

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Bring the water to a boil
  2. Add the eggs
  3. Let it boil for 8 minutes
  4. Drain the hot water and add cold water
  5. Peel of the shell
  6. Cut in halves
  7. Add salt to your liking
